## 1. Overview

Katrin Studio ("the Studio", "we") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights under the Israeli Privacy Protection Law (5741-1981).

## 2. Information We Collect

When you submit the contact form on our website, we collect:

- **Full name**
- **Email address**
- **Phone number**
- **Message content** you provide

We do **not** collect payment details, medical information, or any other sensitive data through the website.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely to:

- Get back to you within 24 hours to schedule your first session
- Answer questions submitted through the form
- Provide information about our services that you requested

We do **not** sell, rent, or transfer your details to third parties for marketing purposes.

## 4. Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. We take reasonable measures to protect it against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or deletion.

Retention period: Contact details are kept for as long as required to provide the service, and no longer than 3 years after the last interaction.

## 5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:

- **Email delivery** — an email infrastructure provider (solely for receiving form submissions)
- **Traffic analytics** — if analytics tools are used (e.g., Google Analytics), this is done without storing personally identifiable information

Any data sharing is subject to confidentiality obligations on the receiving party.

## 6. Your Rights

Under Israeli privacy law, you have the right to:

- **Access** the information collected about you
- **Correct** inaccurate information
- **Request deletion** of your data

To exercise your rights, contact us at: [katrinyair@gmail.com](mailto:katrinyair@gmail.com)

## 7. Cookies

The website may use technically necessary cookies required for proper operation (e.g., storing accessibility preferences). No marketing cookies are used without explicit consent.
