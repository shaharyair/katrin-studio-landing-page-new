## 1. Our Commitment

Katrin Studio is committed to ensuring equal access to our website and services for everyone, including people with disabilities. This site is designed to conform to **Israeli Standard 5568 Part 1 (WCAG 2.0 Level AA)**, in accordance with the **Equal Rights for People with Disabilities Law (5758-1998)** and its accessibility regulations.

## 2. What Is Accessible

The following accessibility features are implemented:

- **Keyboard navigation**, the entire site can be navigated without a mouse
- **Screen reader compatibility**, semantic HTML and ARIA labels are used throughout
- **Colour contrast**, contrast ratios meet Level AA requirements
- **Flexible text sizing**, text can be enlarged without breaking the layout
- **Text alternatives for images**, all informative images include descriptive alt text

### Built-in Accessibility Widget

The site includes a floating accessibility button (wheelchair icon) that provides:

- Font size increase and decrease
- High contrast mode
- Colour inversion
- Grayscale mode
- Link highlighting

Preferences are saved between visits.

## 3. Areas Still Being Improved

We are continuing to improve accessibility. The following areas are in progress:

- Any PDF attachments added in the future will be made accessible
- Future video content will include captions
